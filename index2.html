m5 cs

The "M5 CS" refers to the BMW M5 Competition Sport, a high-performance, limited-edition variant of the BMW M5. It's known for being the most powerful production BMW ever, boasting a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing 635 horsepower. The "CS" designation signifies its focus on track-oriented performance and lightweight design.
